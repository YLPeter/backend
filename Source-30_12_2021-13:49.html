How Disgust Explains Everything

Psychologists will tell you there are six basic emotions. Anger. Surprise. Fear. Enjoyment. Sadness. And disgust. We can think of a few more. But let’s just go with it for now. Of the six, we probably think about disgust the least. Yet, we probably experience it the most. A whiff of a garbage truck. A tragic smear of roadkill. A clogged shower drain. The guy leaving the bathroom without washing his hands. A maskless cough. A cheap scented candle. Sorry. We didn’t mean to gross you out. We just got on a roll and couldn’t stop.  One smart professor decided to study disgust. And it explains a lot.
